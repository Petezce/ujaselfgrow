About Ujasiri

We are a passionate youth group dedicated to empowering young people with the skills and knowledge they need to achieve their full potential.
We believe that every young person deserves the opportunity to develop their talents and make a positive impact on their communities.




Our Programs

LIFE TRANSFORMATIVE TRAINING

Learn health dimensions, conflict resolution, Drug and Substance Abuse.

CAREER DEVELOPMENT

Develop your Technical skills, Upskilling skill,Innovation of products and Employability skills.

Entrepreneurship & Business Skills

Learn the fundamentals of business, Resource mobilization, Finacial management and how to launch your own venture.

ACQUIRE HANDS-ON SKILLS

Learn the Technical skills such as: Plumbing, Electrical and and Electronic, Beauty Therapy.
